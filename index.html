Alzheimer Disease

Alzheimer's disease is a progressive neurological disorder that leads to memory loss and cognitive decline. It is characterized by the accumulation of amyloid plaques and tau tangles in the brain, leading to neuronal death.

This database provides critical information on Alzheimer's disease, including key genes and proteins involved, post-translational modifications (PTMs), cellular localization of proteins, and 3D structures.
